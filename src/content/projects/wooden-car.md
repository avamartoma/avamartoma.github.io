## Overview

This rapid-prototyping challenge focused on **mechanical linkages** and
**structural constraints**. Our team was tasked with designing and fabricating
a functional, **life-sized, human-powered wooden vehicle** within a tight
deadline.

Operating under **strict material limitations** and using **basic machine shop
equipment**, we engineered a reliable **mechanical steering linkage** and
**chassis**.
