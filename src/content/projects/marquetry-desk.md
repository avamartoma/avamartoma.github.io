## Overview

A **wooden foldable desk** I made for my brother using **wood veneer and
marquetry**. I designed the piece in **Fusion**, then built the wood in the
machine shop — veneering the large flat main piece (an **MDF substrate**) with
**walnut** and other veneers. A handmade gift.
