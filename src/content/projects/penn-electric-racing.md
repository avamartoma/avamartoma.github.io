## Overview

For my work with **Penn Electric Racing (PER)** this year, I contributed to
various vehicle subsystems — designing **mounting tabs**, **manufacturing
jigs**, and generating **production-ready technical drawings**.

Because many of these components are deeply integrated into the full-vehicle
master assembly, they're best represented through the standalone **SolidWorks
engineering drawings** I finalized for production.

## Composite airfoils (rookie project)

For my rookie project, I focused extensively on the **composite manufacturing
and layup process** for the aerodynamic airfoils.
