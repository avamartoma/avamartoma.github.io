## Overview

For this project, I designed and optimized a **structural bridge** intended for
**MDF fabrication**. Using **Autodesk Fusion**, I modeled the geometry and
conducted **Finite Element Analysis (FEA)** to simulate load distribution,
stress concentrations, and deflection under structural testing limits.
